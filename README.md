#  MineSweeperCS
Minesweeper that is written on C#

# Usage
```MineSweeper```<br>
Play on a `10x5` field with `15%` mines

`MineSweeper 15`<br>
Play on a `10x5` field with `15%` mines

`MineSweeper 20 10`<br>
Play on a `10x10` field with `20%` mines

`MineSweeper 10 6 8`<br>
Play on a `6x8` field with `10%` mines
